Help us raise Awareness!

The more the merrier! Frequent blood Donors assure us that they feel physically rejuvenated and mentally more fulfilled after every blood donation. There are also reports that it is good to donate blood regularly to prevent some heart diseases. In spite of all the above advantages and probably due to lack of awareness about blood donation, still only about 5% of eligible Donors donate blood.

Blood Requirement Circumstances


1) Accident victims.
2) Patients undergoing all major surgeries.
3) Patients of Thalassemia.
4) All types of cancer patients.
5) Burn victims.
6) Women during complicated childbirth or excessive bleeding or severe anemia or hysterectomy and other surgeries in case of tubal pregnancies.
7) Patients with Bleeding Tendencies.
8) Blood is needed for bleeding injuries and life-saving operations. By donating blood, you may make the difference between life and death of a needy individual.
